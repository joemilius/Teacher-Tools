How to Get the Embed Link Steps

  1. Create a Google Slide Show
  2. Click File and hover over Share
  3. From the dropdown menu choose "Publish to the Web"
  4. A page will pop up and be defaulted to "Link", click on "Embed"
  5. Copy the all text from the text box, it starts with "iframe"
  6. Paste the text in the Embed Form on Teacher Tools and submit
